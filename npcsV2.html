Gidweline

“Pack it up boys, we’re going back!”

Tags: Hartshorn Trismegistus Common Folk

To note, for most of the party, Gidweline was the first person they had seen upon first waking up in the desert.

Professor Gregor A.

“Each step feels heavier than the last: my footfalls toll my final hour. I walk up the flimsy, rotted wooden stairs and a noose is fastened around my neck.”

Tags: Hartshorn Alchemist Association Researcher Iszyre

Professor Gregor A. was one of the founding fathers and the backbone of the Mist Engine project and initial development. After the incident at the Agate Lab, control over the project slipped out of his hands and production of the trains was left in the hands of the newly created Trismegistus and its investors. Suspicious of its rapid development, he formed a team of mercenaries to look into it, ultimately culminating into his incrimination and private hanging.

Last Seen: Oasizh, 30 years ago

Grendell

“I see it my dreams... every night, until I paint them.”

Tags: Roaming City of Crossroads Independent Artist

Grendell is a controversial painter whose existing works always only consist of one painting, which is displayed in the Midnight Gallery of the Qilin House of Culture. Her portfolio includes work such as "The Ultimate Being", "The King od Undeath", "The Returning Flare" and most recently, "The Siren in the Sky". The party took on one of Zirelios' requests, which was to confirm her presence in the City of Crossroads

Last Seen: Qilin, City of Crossroads
