A long, long time ago

  • A Lost God perishes. From their cadaver, a continent is slowly formed.

A long time ago

  • Several rotten individuals are invited for a tour of the Sardior Range’s dragon symposium. In the events that followed, one inherits the white dragon’s flesh, and the others are torn from the great tree of time.

100-500 years ago

  • The Great Migration occurs in Al Ahzoth.
  • The Alchemist Association is officiated.
  • The Corco family moves away from their ancestral home in Pendrath and establish themselves in the City of Crossroads.

30-100 years ago

  • Tragedy strikes as a group of miners digs too deep. The Rubedo mines are boarded up and closed for good.
  • The Aeternum institute loans Nemo-IV to the Alchemist Association.
  • The first combat tournament occurs as part of the Hartshorn Year's End festival.
  • The Well's Chosen, with the help of Romnil, founds Trismegistus.

5-30 years ago

  • Professor Gregor of the Alchemist Association suspects foul play in the Mist Engine's rapid development and hires a group of mercenaries to investigate. As a result:
    • Gregor vanishes from the gallows, never to be seen again.
    • Jax passes away, hung for crimes he did not commit.
    • Folish Ruki and Kai Shi disappear, never to be seen again.
    • Heinrich Swiftfellow and Adra the Turbulent go into hiding.
  • Ylvan Rooksol and Iszyre ingest poison together and pass away.
  • Sir Myrmidlan is removed from Trismegistus after disgracing himself.
  • The Shi family of the Sardior Range start to lose ground to their rivals, the Lu family.
  • Professor Gregor's journal someome finds itself in secure hands in the Oasizh Underground. With it is a note to give it to a man named "Iszyre" should he appear asking for it.
  • The Black Records are released to select parties by the Aeternum Institute.
  • Ylvan Rooksol's Ella Prima Materia is published. His reputation quickly skyrockets, and he becomes Director of the Alchemist Association.
  • The Frontier Campground of Genesizh is extablished.
  • The distribution of Verana leaves begins throughout the Hartshorn Desert.

5 years ago - December 12th 952

  • Morning Lark passes away.
  • Voxanderthol Xeliore is beheaded and passes away.
  • Cadenza Ama’ati is bludgeoned to death from behind and passes away.
  • Endhal, also known as Metta’ain, the End of Hallows, passes away.
  • Syn is offered to the flames and passes away.
  • Tiresius Vinea speaks to party members as they each lie in a mausoleum, chains binding them to a stone bed. Slowly, he learns each of their stories.
  • Zirelios Xeliore wakes up in Al Ahzoth. Trusting that his brother is still alive somewhere and unwilling to start again from nothing, he robs the White Dragon of the Sardior Range blind. He then makes a reputation for himself as an informant of doubtless skill.
  • Darnic Sharpshank starts to notice the rapid decay of his territory… and of himself. He and his counterpart both understand what is to come.
  • Cadenza’s friends wake up in Al Ahzoth with her name on their lips. They decide to split up and hopefully eventually find her.
    • Antonin Scalesong starts to make a name for himself in Oasizh.
    • Solara Sibelius gets a teaching position at the Qilin House of Culture.
  • Alf-Adard wins the Hartshorn Year's End tourney and discards his childhood name.
  • On the day where a certain ritual took place, seven figures rose from certain death, wandered the halls of the Mausoleum of Wills, and followed an unknown impulse to be the last one standing in a battle of grit and instinct.
  • Dangerous creatures start to appear around the Corco Estate.
  • The town of Blastmarrow meets its end. High elf Lanar manages to escape before being discovered.
  • In the City of Crossroads, talks about bringing Sardior Range representatives to the council begins.
